What is it about?

Good question! We like to do our part in building a solid, fun tech community. An Interesting Day is about gathering spectacular people to a full day of great stories, inspiration and just hanging out and having a good time. We gathered top-notch speakers to tell their stories and arranged fun activities throughout the day. We closed off by throwing a party.

Who is invited?

In addition to a lot of great people from the tech and startup scene in San Diego, CA and TIjuana, BC, we invited some of the most brilliant entrepreneurs and creatives from across the world. We aimed to get a really fun and diverse crowd of ~150 people, and people from all backgrounds came to Tijuana to join us.
